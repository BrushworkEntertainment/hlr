HALF-LIFE 2

The Freeman has fallen!

"What if?" - That question stands at the heart of Half-Life 2: Reflections Story. Through a completely original 4-hour campaign we plan to let players experience an alternate reality of the Half-Life universe. Using an entirely new, more slow-paced and puzzle-focused gameplay style with its own quirks and mechanics, you get to experience Half-Life in a new, but eerily familiar, light.
